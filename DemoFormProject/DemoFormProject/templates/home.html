viruses

viruses are small infectious agents that replicates only inside living cells of organisms. viruses can infect all types of life forms from animals to plants to microorganisms. they are found wherever there is life and have probably existed since living cells firs evolved. the origin of viruses is unclear because they do not come from fossils.

coronavirus

Coronavirus disease is an infection disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 (related to the SARS virus). The disease was caused from bats and snakes that were sold in one of china's markets and passes from one person to another usually during coughing or sneezing. The firs case of corona was in December 2019 in Wuhan, China. The case fatality rate is estimated at between 1% and 3%.
